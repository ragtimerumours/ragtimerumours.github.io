An energizing mix of old-skool ragtime, gypsyjazz and roots/blues, played with a rock ‘n roll attitude: "Rag ‘n Roll!"

After a year of busking, gigging The Netherlands and doing a few European tours we're going to represent our country in Memphis, Tennessee at the Worldwide Blues Challenge this January.

We're currently working on another big project, our 16 song debut album “Comic And Other Songs For Smokey Concerts”, which will be released in March.

Let the ragtime's roll!!
