An energizing mix of old-skool ragtime, gypsyjazz and roots/blues, played with a rock ‘n roll attitude: "Rag ‘n Roll!"

After a year of busking, gigging The Netherlands and doing a few European tours we're very proud to say we won 1st place on the European Blues Challenge.

On April 24 we will release our second album Abandon ship.Our new single Yummy & Steve in gypsy style is a limited edition, so get your hands on it now! Our first album Rag'n Roll and EP Ain't Nobody are still available.
